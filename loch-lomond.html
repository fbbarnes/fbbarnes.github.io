Loch Lomond

James, Slava, and I ventured out to Loch Lomond to see the Trossachs but became accidental trainspotters...

We came across a pair of trainspotters who were highly enthused by the Blue Pullman. Apparently the first high speed train to pass through the Highlands.

After our day in the country we headed back to the capital for a few drams... The next afternoon we took a stroll through the city and up to Arthur's Seat.
