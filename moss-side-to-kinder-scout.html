Moss Side to Kinder Scout 2021

We set out to walk from Moss Side to Kinder Scout in a single day. The route took us through the urban landscape of Manchester and Stockport, along the River Goyt and over the Marple Aqueduct, across the Western border of the Peak District National Park, and up to the iconic peak of Kinder Scout. With me were my brother, Christian, and friend, Darioush. Together, we walked over 30 miles in twelve hours from sunrise to sunset.
